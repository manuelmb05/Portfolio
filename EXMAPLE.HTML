Projects

Football & Design: Messi’s Arrival in Toronto

8 min read · Mar 16, 2024

Project Overview and Objective

The fusion of football and Toronto’s urban scene comes alive in this project, using brutalist design to showcase Messi’s visit with Miami FC. Through sports posters and an animation, this visual narrative captures the excitement of the match and its connection with urban culture.

Creative Process

Brutalist elements, geometric shapes, and sharp angles converge to create dynamic posters. Using Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator, the visual identity of the teams and the city were meticulously crafted, with textures adding depth and an urban touch.

Poster and Animation

In addition to the posters, the billboard animation conveys the message of Messi’s arrival in less than 7 seconds, blending brutalist style with neon effects and abstract elements.
