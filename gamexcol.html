As a passionate multimedia artist with a penchant for evoking nostalgia, I embarked on an exciting journey alongside Game X Col, an independent video game company rooted in Colombia. Our mission? To revive the essence of the '90s through a logo and animation that would resonate with today's gamers while tapping into the fond memories of yesteryears. Inspired by Colombia's rich biodiversity, we chose the iconic Bothrops atrox snake to symbolize the company's identity. From exhilarating brainstorming sessions to meticulous typographic exploration and improvised animation, every step of our creative process was fueled by a deep passion for innovation and a profound respect for our heritage. I warmly invite you to delve into this creative odyssey, where client vision converges with my design expertise to breathe life into something truly unique. Come, join us in exploring our creative process and discover how we crafted something extraordinary together.
