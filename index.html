Game X Col

See animation

I worked on an exciting project for Game X Col, a Colombian video game company. They wanted a fresh visual identity, so I created a logo and animation with a '90s vibe, infused with Colombian elements and a modern twist. Inspired by gaming giants like Capcom and Nintendo, along with Colombia's unique biodiversity, we used Adobe Illustrator and After Effects to bring the vision to life.

True Spotify

Scan the code

True, a Colombian fashion brand, aimed to innovate in the presentation of its latest collection, TRUE x SPOTIFY, by merging fashion and augmented reality. Their goal was to offer a unique experience where guests could explore the collection using QR codes, view garments inspired by iconic albums, and connect each outfit with the most popular song from the corresponding album on Spotify.
