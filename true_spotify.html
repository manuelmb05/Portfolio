Welcome to a unique experience at the intersection of fashion and technology! With TRUE x SPOTIFY, we've crafted something truly special. Before diving into our world of creativity, I invite you to scan the QR code and unlock an experience that will change your perception of fashion and music. In collaboration with TRUE, a pioneering brand in Colombian fashion, we set out to merge fashion with augmented reality technology in an innovative way. Using tools like Adobe AERO, Substance 3D, Illustrator, and Photoshop, we transformed flat designs into dynamic 3D models, bringing to life an interactive showcase where you can explore garments inspired by iconic albums. From conception to realization, our creative process has been a journey of collaboration, meticulous modeling and texturing, and seamless integration of Spotify music. I invite you to discover how we brought this vision to life and to immerse yourself in our creative process!
