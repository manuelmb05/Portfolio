Embark on a transformative journey with the Multimedia Design for Conscious Travel project: Innovative Posters in York Region Transit! In this endeavor, my aim was to merge the creativity of illustration with augmented reality technology to craft impactful posters that not only grab attention but also promote civic responsibility on buses in Ontario, Canada. As a student of Multimedia Design and Development at Humber College, I set out to address behavioral issues and raise awareness among commuters about proper etiquette. Join me by scanning the QR codes and immerse yourself in an interactive experience that transcends traditional poster designs! At the end of this journey, I invite you to delve deeper into the creative process behind these innovative posters, exploring the evolution from concept to realization, and discovering how multimedia technology can drive positive change in our communities.
