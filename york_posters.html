This project captures Messi's arrival in Toronto by blending football with urban art, using brutalist style to reflect the city's vibrant energy. Featuring sports posters and a billboard animation, it showcases geometric shapes and monochromatic colors to convey a raw, authentic urban vibe. Targeted at both football fans and young people engaged in urban culture, the design incorporates tones representing Miami FC and Toronto FC, along with iconic elements from the city. The billboard animation, inspired by Dundas Square advertising, delivers the visual message powerfully in just a few seconds.
